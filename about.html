Welcome to S.P.M

Who We Are

Strategic Prayer Ministries is a daily apostolic and prophetic platform where people come to experience the Word, Prayer, and Healing. The Spirit of intercession upon the ministry enables believers to build their prayer lives and brings them into true intimacy with the Holy Spirit.

Our Mission

Strategic Prayer Ministries exists to raise up a generation of believers who are passionate about prayer, intimacy with God, and advancing His kingdom through strategic intercession.

We believe that prayer is not just a religious activity, but a powerful weapon that changes atmospheres, transforms lives, and brings heaven to earth.

God has given us a mission to raise people of prayer and set hearts on fire for God.

Our Values

Prayer

Prayer is our foundation and lifeline, connecting us to God's presence and guiding everything we do.

Love

We are called to love God and people, showing kindness and selflessness in every action.

Excellence

In all things, we give our best to honor God and inspire others.

Charity

We extend compassion through generosity, serving the poor with Christ's heart of love.

Our Vision

We envision a global movement of prayer warriors who are equipped, empowered, and positioned to see breakthrough in their lives, communities, and nations. We believe God is raising up an army of intercessors who will usher in the greatest revival the world has ever seen.

Our heart is to see believers move from casual Christianity to radical discipleship, from powerless religion to Spirit-filled living, and from individual faith to corporate unity that demonstrates the love and power of Jesus Christ.

Join the Movement

Whether you are just beginning in prayer or you are a seasoned intercessor, there is a place for you here. Encounter God's presence in intimate prayer, receive fresh prophetic insight, and grow with a community hungry to see His kingdom come.
